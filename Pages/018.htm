You and Wendy find the tool kit, then watch in amazement as Sam unpacks a bunch of steel parts. He sticks inflatable wings through slots in the sides and rear of the trailer. "Now, the propeller... the propeller," he mutters. "Ah, here it is."

The trailer begins to spin out of control. "Hurry!" Wendy urges him.

Sam steps into the driver's seat and presses a button. You hear the buzz of a small engine.

Ethel walks through the trailer. "Please return to your seats, the captain has turned on the 'fasten seat belt' sign," she requests.

Wow! Sam did it! He turned the trailer into a plane!

"Careful," Ethel warns you. "We may experience some turbulence."

The trailer begins to shake violently. You glance at two empty seats. You and Wendy sit and strap yourselves in. From the driver's seat Sam yells back, "Hold on!"

The flying trailer starts to bounce and roll. You grip the sides of the chair so tightly, your knuckles turn white.

Will Sam be able to fly out of this?
