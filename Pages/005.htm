The fierce command halts the wind. Dust settles and you flop down on the cave floor.

What is going on here? you wonder. Who is talking now? A noise disturbs your thoughts. You notice Wendy stirring. She sits up and rubs the side of her head.

"What's happening?" Wendy croaks.

"I don't know," you whisper. "Something stopped the storm from smashing us. Let's cut out of here while we can."

"No way!" Wendy says. "We came to give back the doll."

You gaze at the doll in your hand. "Well, what's the deal? We're in the cave, what do we have to do now?"

"Beats me," Wendy replies. "I'm positive this is the right cave, but..." She touches the bump on her scalp. "I feel confused from getting conked. I'm sure there's something else we should do. I just can't remember what."

You still think it's time to leave the doll and run, but Wendy has been right about this stuff before.

Then again, her memory is a little messed up right now. What should you do?
