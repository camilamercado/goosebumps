"Yes," you reply, "so do I. But first, I want to know if you really are the voice of this mighty tornado. Can you make the twister move?"

"Of course!" the voice answers. It sounds annoyed.

"Can you prove it?" you ask. "Could you move to the east about a half a mile?"

"You mean, into the electromagnetic field?" the voice booms. The face of blue light flickers with rage. It bears its teeth.

"But - I-" you try to explain.

"You fool!" the voice replies. "I am the master of wind, sound, and air! And I can hear your thoughts."

"What's wrong?" Sally's question echoes in your ear.

But you can't answer. The wind shakes your storm suit - hard. The communication antenna snaps off.

With a sickening squeal of metal, the suit splits in half. All your protection is gone!

"You think you can tame the wind?" the storm cries. "You will become the wind!"
