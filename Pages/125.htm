You stretch your legs as far as they go. The tip of your big toe just reaches the intercom switch. It clicks on. You take a deep breath and start to talk as loudly as you can.

"One hundred million dollars!" you cry. "I can't believe it! And all for you? How are you going to keep Sally from getting her share?"

"What?" Spin asks. "I never..."

In a piercing voice you interrupt him. "I've got to hand it to you, Spin. She'll never know you're going to cut her loose in the twister until it's too late."

You cross your fingers, hoping Sally can hear every word you're saying.

Spin glances down at the control panel - and notices that you've turned on the intercom. "Why, you little sneak!" he growls.

"Spin, you double-Grosser!" Sally's voice is loud and angry. "I heard everything! You won't get away with this!"

Spin leans toward the intercom. "Sally! No! Wait!" he pleads.
