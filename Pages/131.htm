"Aaaaaah!" you scream.

The tires on the van squeal. The couple jumps back - as the van stops right in front of them.

Whew! The man opens the driver's side door. The woman jumps into the passenger's side. You hop into the back.

I am never, ever driving again till I get a license! you promise yourself.

The man stomps on the gas pedal and jerks the wheel over. The van spins around and zooms away from the oncoming twister. You bounce onto a dirt road and start putting miles between you and the deadly storm. Finally he pulls over to the side of the road. You still see the tornado in the rearview mirror, but it's not headed toward you.

The man claps you on the back. "You did it!" he shouts with joy. "You saved our lives!"
