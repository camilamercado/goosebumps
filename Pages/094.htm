Hey! You're just like Superman. And you're not the only one pretending to be an action hero - Wendy whizzes by you, waving.

"Want to play too?" she calls. Then she flies away.

Play? What's she talking about? you wonder. But you don't have much time to think about it. Something is coming your way. It's long and green and hangs straight up and down. Is it a ladder? Or a rope? Maybe - if you stretch - you can grab it as you fly by.

"Hey!" A voice shouts at you. Your heart jumps. You turn your head and see a boy flying next to you!

"Do you want to play or what?" the boy asks.

Wait. Where did he come from? you think. And what does he mean, play?

You want to ask, but you're approaching the rope fast. You've got only a split second to choose.

If you grab the rope, go to PAGE 75.

If you ask the boy what he means, go to PAGE 31.
