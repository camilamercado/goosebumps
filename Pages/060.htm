"Give me the doll!" the voice of the tornado demands.

"No!" you shout. You try to sound brave, but you're beginning to lose your nerve. Sweat beads on your forehead. "I'm supposed to return this to the cave spirit - not to you!"

"You dare to deny me!" the voice roars. You wince in pain as the sound vibrates through your entire body. The force of the wind picks up. You feel it tearing at you, turning you around. You try to shield Wendy from it, but both of you are lifted like feathers. You bang against the cave's stone walls with a painful jolt.

This is it! you think. It's the end!

But now you hear another voice. This one is also loud, but calmer and gentler.

"Stop!" it commands.
