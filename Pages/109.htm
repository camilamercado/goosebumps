You don't know who that man and woman are, but you can't let them walk into the storm. You have to save them!

You race toward them as fast as you can. Your heart pounds and your breath comes in gasps.

But they're farther away than you realized. You're still too far to shout to them!

You continue on - and notice an abandoned van in the field. Hey! You could try to drive to the couple and take them far away before the twister hits!

There's only one problem - you don't know how to drive! You could crash and really hurt someone.

Should you get in the van and go for it?
