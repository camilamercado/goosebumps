It's a big risk, trusting these two strangers, but you'll do anything to save your aunt and uncle.

"Sure! I'll help you!" you declare.

"Great!" Spin replies. "Let's go to our lab."

While Spin drives, Sally explains the project. The two scientists have built a "storm suit" - a one-person suit of armor, with jet packs on the back, attached to electronic controls by a long cable. They plan to launch it into a twister - with someone inside!

Whoever's in the suit should be able to explore the safety zone the scientists have discovered. And, they think, the suit will enable a person to control the storm!

Wow! you think. An invention like that could keep a tornado from destroying towns and people!

"Maybe we'll find your aunt and uncle inside the tornado - and rescue them," Spin says. You smile at the thought.

"One of us would do it," Sally tells you. "But the suit is kind of small. We'd have a better chance if someone your size were inside it."

You gulp. "Wait a minute. Do you mean...?"
