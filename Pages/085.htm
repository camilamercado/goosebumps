"Help!" you scream. The wind spins you around in the air, first slowly, then faster and faster. Soon you're lifted out of the cave and into the air above the mountain.

You stop spinning as the wind floats you over the countryside. You gaze down. Whoa! Before your eyes the damage done by the twister is being magically undone. Trees are placed upright. Buildings are magically pieced together.

Then you're over your own farm. You watch as everything returns to the way it was before the twister hit.

Wendy said the wind spirit would restore everything to normal once the doll was returned. Now she's keeping her promise! you realize.

You're lowered to the ground in front of your aunt and uncle's farmhouse. And the two of them are there! Sitting on the front porch! They're alive! You run to them.

"Where have you been?" your uncle asks. "I was getting worried."

Your aunt and uncle probably don't remember anything about the twister, you realize. Better not to remind them.

"I was just hanging out with some friends," you answer. "And we were, uh - totally into this game called Twister!"

THE END
