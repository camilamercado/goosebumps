There's no time to waste. You have to take a chance with this bridge.

"Come on, boy!" you order Yoyo. "I'll go first. If it holds my weight, it will hold yours, no problem."

You try not to look at the muddy, swollen river as it rages under the bridge, just inches from your feet. Slowly, carefully, you feel your way out onto the span. It shudders and shakes from the water pounding at it. But then - you're across! Safe on dry ground.

"You can make it, Yoyo!" you shout.

Yoyo runs lightly across the bridge. Yes! He did it!

He leads you up a large hill. As you reach the top, a deafening roar stops you in your tracks. You turn toward it - and gasp! The entire bridge has been washed out by a surge of water! Whew! Good thing you crossed it in time!

"Let's go," Yoyo insists. "Your aunt and uncle are close by"
