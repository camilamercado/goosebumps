"Sally!" Spin yells into the radio. "It's time! Are you ready to control the storm?"

Sally's voice comes back over the wire. You can barely make it out over the sound of the howling wind.

"It's harder than I thought to control this thing," she shouts. "The suit's storm capture device is working well, but I need you to boost my steering power."

"Fine!" Spin shouts. He sprints to the far side of the control panel. He flips switches and turns dials. "Just destroy that town!"

You see the round, swirling shape of the storm moving across the radar screen. As Sally gains control of it, it slowly changes direction.

You know where it's headed - Tully's Creek. Soon the whole town, including your friends, will be wiped out! Unless you do something about it.

But how?
