"Can you hear me?"

A voice stirs you from a deep sleep. Slowly you open your eyes. You look up into the kind face of a doctor. He's surrounded by an ambulance crew and police officers.

"Wh-what happened?" you stammer.

"Why, you saved Tully's Creek!" the doctor answers.

"We heard the whole thing on our radios," a state trooper next to him tells you. "Including the way you tricked those two crazy scientists into ruining their plan!"

"Spin and Sally. Where are they?" you ask.

"No sign of them," the policeman answers. "You were lucky. You landed in some bushes over there."

"You saved an entire town!" the doctor cheers. "You're a hero."

The next day you take part in a parade in your honor down Tully's Creek's Main Street. Every townsperson turns out for the festivities. So no one is home to hear the weather report.
