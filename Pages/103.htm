"These trailer people are too weird!" you tell Wendy. "We've got to save ourselves!"

You glance around the trailer. There must be something that will get you out of this mess. But what? You spot a door leading to another room.

"In here!" you call to Wendy.

You open the door and step into a small storage room. It's piled with boxes, tools, and spare parts.

"Cool!" you cry. "We can use some of this stuff!"

"For what?" Wendy snorts. "To stop a tornado?"

Before you can answer, you shudder. Your eardrums are assaulted by the sound of tearing metal.

CREEEAACK!

Your eyes go wide. The entire trailer is splitting in two! The half with Sam and Ethel and the kids is carried away by the twister. It goes soaring up into the sky. But the half you and Wendy are in plunges toward the ground!

"Yowwwwwww!" you scream.
