The wind gently lowers you and Wendy.

"I know this place!" you shout. "It's our farm! I'm home! The wind spirit blew us back here as a reward!"

"This was a farm?" Wendy asks with doubt in her voice.

You glance around at the ruined house and barn. You remember your missing aunt and uncle, and your joy vanishes.

"The lousy twister," you mutter.

"Hey! What's that?" Wendy points up into the sky.

Whoa! You can't believe your eyes. It's your aunt and uncle! They're flying through the air too!

"This must be the wind spirit's reward!" you shout.

"Uncle Jed!" you call. You run to them after they land. "Aunt Emily! What happened to you? Where were you?"

"I don't know," your uncle answers. "But I sure did like the in-flight movie!"

"That was the wind spirit," you start to explain, but your aunt interrupts to give you a big hug.

"It doesn't matter," she says. "We're all together again."

"Yeah, Aunt Emily," you agree. "There's no place like home!"

THE END
