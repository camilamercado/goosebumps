Sam and Ethel know something. You're sure of it. And you have an idea. The trailer dives, sending the couch sliding past you. You grab the remote control from Sam.

"Hey, give that back!" Sam howls.

In one breath you blurt out, "We're-caught-in-a-twister-and-we're-all-going-to-be-killed-unless-we-get-out-of-here!" As if to make your point cleared the trailer takes another sickening dive before swooping up again.

"In a twister? Why didn't you say so?" Ethel remarks. "Honey, where'd you put that airplane conversion kit?"

Sam gets up from the couch and walks to the back of the trailer. "It's in here somewhere, I think."

"Airplane conversion kit?" you repeat, stunned.

"Advertised on TV" Ethel nods. "A real bargain."

"I knew it would come in handy one day," Sam grunts. He pulls a large carton out of a storage closet.

The trailer takes a stomach-twisting dive. "Whatever you're doing," you plead, "could you please hurry?"

"Sure," Sam answers. "Did anyone see my tool kit?"
