"My name is Dr. Neederlander, and this is my niece, Wendy, and my nephew, Peter," the white-haired old man tells you. "We were just playing a little game that I invented called 'Twister Tag.' "

"Twister Tag?" you echo.

"It's a lot like hide-and-seek," Neederlander explains. "I was 'it' so I played the twister. It was my job to get Wendy and Peter and suck them into the storm. It's really scary when you're playing, but totally harmless."

"Wait. How could you be the twister?" you ask. Then you see it - a huge jet pack strapped on to Neederlander's back.

"That's right." He chuckles, noticing your expression. "I do it with this. One of my inventions, the Twister Pack." He points to his back. "Unfortunately our game got a little out of hand. Your aunt and uncle got swept up in it."

He frowns. "I'm really sorry I wrecked your house. But I am a famous inventor. I told your aunt and uncle I'd pay for a new house. If they agree to play the game with us."

You shake your head. This is too much to take in.

"So, what do you think?" Neederlander asks. "Would you like to play too?"
