The twirling cone shoots into the cave. You brace yourself, ready to be slammed into the wall.

But the wind doesn't hurt you - it surrounds you! You and Wendy are inside the twister. How can this be?

"I am the storm!" a deep voice booms out of nowhere. Your eardrums throb with pain from the sound. "I am summoned by the wind spirit in this cave."

"Why are you chasing us?" you demand. "We only want to return the doll to the wind spirit."

"Give me the doll and I will return it," the voice thunders. "Then I will stop following you!"

"You?" you ask, puzzled.

"Yes!" the voice booms. "Throw it into my whirlwind!"

All right! If you throw the doll into the storm, this whole nightmare will end!

But - wait. Didn't Wendy say the doll had to be returned to the place she stole it from? She didn't say anything about giving it to the storm. What should you do?
