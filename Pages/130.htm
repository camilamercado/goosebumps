The two of you climb on Oatbucket. "Git!" you shout. But the horse is so worn out, he can only manage a trot.

You squint off into the distance. Hey! There's a town up ahead! If you can get there before the twister, you might find shelter.

Oatbucket plods ahead so slowly, you almost think you can run faster. "Move," you urge him, but the tornado gains on you.

Finally you reach the town. You gaze around, and your heart sinks. It's in ruins. The only building still standing is an old furniture store.

The sky grows dark. In the distance you hear a booming sound. The twister is coming for you!

Your skin prickles with fear. "The storm's coming too fast! We've got to find shelter!" you yell to Wendy over the roar of the approaching storm.

Wendy stares at you, wide-eyed. "No! We have to keep going," she insists. "We have to return the doll."
