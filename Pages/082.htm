Flying twigs and gravel pelt you and Yoyo. They scrape your face and hands. The tornado's roar fills your ears.

Have to keep going, you think. Have to make it to safety! You're about to give up from exhaustion, when the wind dies down.

Huh? What happened? You glance around.

Yoyo has led you to a small hill a short distance from the farmhouse. The air here is still. But you can see the twister still raging over your home!

A strange groaning sound fills your ears.

The tornado rushes at your aunt and uncle's house.

"The storm cellar!" you choke. The twister is right over it. Your aunt and uncle are down there!

KA-BOOM! The house explodes from the force of the wind.

"Nooo!" you yell. Yoyo barks. He scrambles away. He wants you to follow him. But - what about your aunt and uncle? Do you stay and see if they're okay? Or do you follow your faithful dog?
