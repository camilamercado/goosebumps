Yoyo will have to take care of himself. You run for the storm cellar with all your might. You leap inside. Your uncle bolts the door right behind you.

The roaring of the storm grows louder and louder. The storm cellar door shakes and rattles.

You're sure the wind will break through and lift the three of you up into the sky. But the solid door holds firm. Soon the noise dies down.

Nervously your uncle takes a peek outside.

The sky is bright blue and birds are chirping. You and your aunt and uncle step out into a sunny day.

And there's Yoyo, running and barking as though nothing had happened. The storm has passed without causing any damage at all!

"Well, that was quite an adventure," your aunt Emily says as she walks back to the house.

"Yep," nods your uncle Jed. "A real adventure!"
