"Wendy!" you shout. You shake her. But it's no use. She isn't conscious. Pebbles and dust start pouring in through the cave entrance. You choke and gag on swirling dust.

Anger wells up inside you. That's it, you decide. You're tired of running from the tornado. You're ending this - right now! You grab the doll from Wendy's pocket and stand up.

"Why are you chasing us?" you scream at the wind. "We're here to return the doll!"

Fuming, you wait for an answer. But instead, you get the twister. Big-time.

Its dark funnel roars up to the entrance of the cave! Its sound is so intense, you have to stick your fingers in your ears. Then, as you watch dumbfounded, the twister snakes its way into the cave!
