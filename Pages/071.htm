"Oatbucket can't carry us much farther," you argue. "We have to try our luck here."

Wendy nods in agreement. The two of you slide off the horse's back. With a whinny he gallops away.

You nod toward the old furniture store. "Maybe there's a cellar in that building!" you guess. You burst through the front door and search, but there's no stairway. Nothing!

A crumpling sound freezes you in your tracks. You watch in horror as the front doors of the building peel off!

The wind reaches in for you. It knocks you flat on the floor. You grab the leg of a heavy table to keep yourself from being sucked into the storm.

"Over here!" Wendy screams. She's sitting behind a pile of carpets. You scramble toward her on your hands and knees.

"We are in deep trouble!" you shout as the building rattles around you. "These old rugs won't save us!"

KA-BOOM! Every window in the place implodes! You bury your face in a rug and wait for the end. But Wendy nudges you with her elbow.

"What?" you snap at her.
