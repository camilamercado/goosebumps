"Yeeow!" you scream. You and Wendy are blown clear out of the cave and hundreds of feet into the air. You tumble head over heels, until you can't tell the sky from the ground.

"Some reward," you yell to Wendy. "We're going to be killed!"

"Maybe the spirit was still really angry!" she wails.

"No kidding," you snap back at her.

You shoot across the sky, held up by a powerful column of air. Then your heart leaps. For the first time since you entered the cave, you feel a glimmer of hope.

"Look!" you shout, pointing to the farmland below. "I think I know what's happening!"
