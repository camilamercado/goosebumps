Slowly the bucking and roaring of the wind dies down. The sky outside the windows clears -you're out of the storm!

Ethel rises from her seat. "The captain has now turned off the 'fasten seat belt' sign," she announces. "Please feel free to move about the trailer."

Whew! You escaped the storm - again! You can't believe your luck!

"Where do you two want to go?" Sam calls. Wendy directs him toward a range of low hills. The trailer dips its nose and lands in a small field. Wendy drags you to the door.

Sam scratches his head. "Problem with a flying trailer is you can't get cable TV," he observes. "Oh, well."

You shrug and follow Wendy outside. "We're safe!" you exclaim.

Wendy stares over her shoulder. "Wrong," she moans. You look in the same direction. Just as Wendy said, the tornado, though far away, seems as if it's still after you!

"To the cave!" Wendy screams. She holds up the doll that she showed you earlier. "We're not far now! Run!"
