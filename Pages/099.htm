Go inside a tornado? The whole idea sounds crazy.

"Uh, thanks, but no thanks," you tell them.

Spin smiles wickedly. "Sorry, we don't take no for an answer." He grabs your arms and twists them behind your back.

"Hey!" you cry. Before you can move, the two of them tie your arms and legs and throw you into the back of the van.

After a while the van screeches to a halt. The back doors open. Spin hoists you up onto his shoulder and carries you into a low gray building.

"Let me go!" you shout.

"Oh, we'll let you go," Sally replies. "In that!" She points out a nearby window. You gasp. Outside is a tall, narrow rocket. It's aimed at the sky.

Sally smiles. "Like it or not, that is how we're sending you into the tornado!"
