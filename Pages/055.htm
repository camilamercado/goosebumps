"You're right. The best way to help that trailer guy," you tell Wendy, "is to return the doll to the cave and stop the twister."

Wendy nods. You follow her as she sprints up a trail. Every time you turn back, the black cone of the tornado is closer - and larger.

Soon it fills the sky. The wind howls around you, tearing at your clothes. Tree branches snap off and fly away. You dodge left and right to avoid them.

You struggle to the top of the rise. "There it is!" Wendy shouts over the noise of the storm.

There's a small opening in the rocks, just a few feet away. You dive into the entrance to the cave, just as the full blast of the storm hits. The funnel hovers just outside the cave. It seems to be trying to follow you in!

"Wendy, the doll!" you declare. "Where do we return it?"
