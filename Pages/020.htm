You pull yourself up the green rope, above the twister!

At the top of the rope, you poke your head through a fleecy cloud - and gasp. In the distance stands a huge castle.

And in front of the castle is - a giant!

No, it can't be, you think. Giants only exist in fairy tales, right? Wrong. The giant lumbers over to you.

You try to slide down the rope. Too late! The giant plucks you off the vine and peers at you with his huge bloodshot eyes.

"Where's my goose?" he roars, setting you down. "Where's my harp? I'll break your bones to bake my bread!"

Now that sounds familiar. You've seen this giant before - in a fairy tale, the one with the bean stalk. Only now, somehow, he's totally real!

You're so scared, you babble the first thing that comes into your mind: "Uh, fee-fie-fo-fum?"

The giant frowns. "What's that supposed to mean? I hate people who make no sense." He brings his foot down - right on top of you! SQUASH!

Too bad! Looks like your choice of last words was fee-fie-fo-DUMB!

THE END
