There's no way you can reach them in time unless you start driving! You race to the van and climb into the driver's seat. Luckily, the key is in the starter! You turn it and the engine roars to life!

Yes! you think. Driving is going to be a snap! Even if your feet barely reach the pedals.

Hmmm. Which pedal is the brake? You stomp on one of them. VRRROOOM! You take off into the field.

Yeeow! The brake must be the other one! you think.

You ease off the gas a little and gain control of the steering. You point the car toward the couple. All right! You're going to do it. You're going to rescue the couple and get away alive!

But then the couple notices you. They start waving and shouting something. But what are they saying? Either they want you to stop - or to step on it. Oh, no. Which is it?

If you step on the brake, turn to PAGE 25.

If you speed up, turn to PAGE 102.
