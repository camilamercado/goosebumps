You stop yourself at the last moment. These animals sound as if they hate humans. They're not going to be too happy that you overheard their secrets. But Yoyo must have known they were in here. Why did he lead you to this house?

And, hey, what happened to Yoyo, anyway?

Quietly you step outside the farmhouse. The storm has passed and the sky is beginning to clear.

There is your dog, Yoyo, sitting by the doorway. You're no longer freaked out when he talks to you. But you are amazed. You stand there with your mouth hanging open.

"Now you know," he says in a low voice.

"Yes." You nod. "I know. Why didn't you ever talk to me before?"

"We can't talk all the time," he explains as he licks his paws. "But something happens to us when a big storm is coming or when an earthquake is about to hit. We gain special powers. And we can talk."

You shake your head. This is so totally weird.

"I'm glad you told me," you say, kneeling down.

"There's something else I wanted to tell you," the dog continues. "Your aunt and uncle are in trouble!"
