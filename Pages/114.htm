Spin's truck is already a small dot on the horizon. And the giant tornado is roaring at you!

Out of the corner of your eye you spot a pile of metal in the grass. Is that what you think it is? You check it out. Yes! It's another storm suit!

Quickly you climb inside and fasten the helmet closed. You're not tied to a cable like Sally was, but maybe the suit will still save your life.

The twister blasts you with its full force. It throws you backwards, and knocks you to the ground. Then it sends you tumbling along the ground like a leaf.

"Owww!" you howl. Your body hurts from slamming into the metal suit, but you're still in one piece.

The world goes black. You can't see anything! You must be in the middle of the funnel! You feel yourself hurtling through the air. Luckily, the suit does its job.

Soon you bump lightly to the ground. The air begins to clear. You look around and see the twister moving away.

Yes! You made it!
