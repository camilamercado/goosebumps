BOOM! A blast carries you into the air. You tumble head over heels. Then, from your waist, you hear the roar of rockets. They stabilize the suit so that you're upright.

You know this would be cool if you didn't have a reason for being here. You peer through the storm, searching for the one thing that matters most to you - your family.

You can't make out anything - except black dust. You glance down at the lights of the instrument panel inside your helmet. The digital readout tells you you're a thousand feet in the air and traveling at one hundred fifty miles an hour!

"What do you see?" Sally's voice rings in your ears.

"Well " You stop short.

"I've been waiting for you!" a voice whispers.

Whoa! Who is that? It doesn't sound like Spin or Sally! You twist around. Where is that voice coming from?

"Did you hear that?" you shout into your microphone.

"Hear what?" Sally replies.

"I don't know," you mutter. You shake your head. Could you have imagined the voice?'

Your spine tingles when you hear it again. "I am the storm," it declares. "I've been waiting for you."
