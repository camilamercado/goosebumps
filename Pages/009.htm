Wendy points into the depths of the cave. "Back there!" she shouts. The wind almost swallows her words.

Together the two of you struggle toward the rear of the tunnel. Hunched over, you fight for every step against the pull of the wind. At any moment the mighty twister could lift you up, draw you out of the cave, and into the sky!

"I can't make it!" Wendy moans.

You swallow. You're not sure if you can make it, either. You glance ahead. In the gloom of the dark cavern, you see a length of old rope coiled on the ground. Hey! You could tie yourselves to one of the large boulders in the cave, then wait out the storm.

But what if this tornado is so strong, it snaps the rope? Maybe you should just keep forging ahead instead!
