Sally rushes toward the twister. You shudder. How can she risk her life in that terrible wind?

Spin taps your shoulder. "Let's get to the controls."

In the back of the truck, you sit in front of a bank of video monitors. You can see a long cable stretching from the truck to Sally's metal suit. It sends you data about her, and it allows you to control parts of the suit.

Totally cool. As you watch, two small jets shoot out of the pack on Sally's back, sending her straight up into the dark clouds.

"Wow!" you exclaim.

"She's up!" Spin cries from the other side of the monitors. "Switch to Sally's camera. Hit button A-1."

You reach out and press A-1. But instead of a view from Sally's camera, you see a close-up of Spin, talking directly into the lens. Must be an old recording, you reason. You start to turn it off, but then Spin says something strange.

"Attention, mayor of Tully's Creek. Our invention has given us control of the tornado. Deliver one hundred million dollars to us by two P.M. or we will direct the tornado to your town and kill you all!"
