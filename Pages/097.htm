"We have to help that man!" you shout. You run for the trailer. Wendy follows at your heels.

The man stands on the trailer doorstep, dressed in jeans and a T-shirt. Weird. He seems totally calm.

"There you are!" he exclaims. "Where's the pizza?"

"Pizza?" you repeat. "What pizza?"

"Aren't you delivering pizzas?" He looks disappointed. "We ordered one about an hour ago."

"No!" you shout. "There's a huge twister coming...."

But the man goes inside the trailer. You and Wendy follow. You have to make him understand he's in danger!

Inside, a woman in a red print dress is sitting on a couch watching television with two kids.

"Is that the pizza, Sam?" she asks.

"No, Ethel," Sam replies. "Just a couple of kids."

The wind starts to howl. The trailer shakes and shudders. You're scared. But the family keeps watching TV

You turn to Wendy. "Explain it! Make them understand!"

"Too late," Wendy groans. Her face has turned pale. "Look out the window. This trailer is... it's flying!"
