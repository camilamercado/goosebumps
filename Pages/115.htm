"WHAT?" you cry. "The whole trailer is flying?"

You glance outside. It's true! You're staring down at the tops of trees!

With a lurch the whole trailer falls, then climbs again. Chairs and tables slide from one wall to the next. "Oof!" you gasp. A stool rams your stomach.

The trailer is pitching and diving. At any moment it could crash to the ground! You've got to get Wendy and the trailer family out of this -somehow.

What's wrong with these people? you think. They're still sitting on their sofa, watching TV!

The only way you'd be calm in a situation like this is... Hey! That's it! Maybe Sam and Ethel know a way out!
