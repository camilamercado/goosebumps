"Sally, you have to believe me," Spin pleads. But there's no answer.

A loud whooshing sound from outside the truck brings a look of fear to his face. He stares at the radar screen. His mouth drops open in shock.

You can see what has him so upset. The tornado is no longer headed toward Tully's Creek. Instead, it's coming straight at the truck - and you! Sally must have gained total control over the storm with her suit. Now she's mad at Spin, and she's coming to get him!

The truck begins to shake from the increasing winds. The walls bang and rattle.

BOOM!

The truck is lifted off the ground. It tilts backwards.

You slam into the back door, nearly breaking your arm.

"Aaaah!" you scream. The door bangs open. You're falling... falling to the ground. You look up and spot the truck being lifted into the sky, with Spin still inside.
