This is too much for you to handle alone. You have to tell Sally about it. You switch on your microphone.

"Sally!" you cry. "You're never going to believe this but the twister is-"

"Talking to you?" the scientist interrupts. "I knew it!" she shouts. "I knew there was an intelligence behind these storms. Now we're going to trap it!"

"Sally," you protest. You glance at the face of the fierce-looking storm. "How can we trap a tornado?"

" Don't worry," Sally reassures you. "We have an electromagnetic field prepared that can hold the twister. All you have to do is get it to the right spot."

You listen carefully to her plan, but you still have doubts about Sally's scheme. Then again, she's a scientist. She must know what she's doing. Right?

You swallow and try talking to the twister again.

"Uh, hello!" you say, trying to sound calm.

"I want to talk to you," the twister snarls.
