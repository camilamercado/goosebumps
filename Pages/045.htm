"That store doesn't look like it can stand up to the twister anyway," you declare. "Let's keep going."

You urge Oatbucket forward.

BOOM!

A bolt of lightning strikes a massive oak tree just yards away. The towering tree splits in half and falls across the road. Oatbucket squeals and rears up on his hind legs. You and Wendy flip to the ground.

"Oatbucket! No!" you shout. But he trots off.

"Come on," Wendy cries. "The cave isn't too far."

She heads for a group of hills in the distance. You start to follow. Until you notice a trailer by the side of the road. A man is standing in the trailer's door, waving frantically at you.

"That guy looks like he needs us," you tell Wendy.

"The only way we can help anyone is by stopping the storm!" she exclaims. "We have to reach the cave!"

The storm is growing closer by the second. Which is the right thing to do?
