Without hesitating you jump down into the pit.

"Don't worry, boy!" you murmur. You crouch by Yoyo's side. "I'll get you out of here, somehow!"

"No, I'll get you out!" he answers. Then he jumps to his hind legs. An orange glow surrounds him.

What is going on? you wonder.

"Don't be afraid!" he declares. "Your bravery and kindness have released my full powers. You see, not only can I sense the storm, but with the aid of a true friend I can also control it!"

You watch, openmouthed, as the dog walks on his hind legs to the center of the pit. He lifts his head skyward. Instantly the wind swirls over you. It gently lifts you and Yoyo out of the pit and onto the ground. Cool!

The twister moves toward the rubble that buried your aunt and uncle. The powerful wind clears the rubble away! It lifts rocks, beams, and entire walls. Yes! At last you see your aunt and uncle - and they're safe!

Yoyo turns to you with a broad, toothy smile.

"Not bad for a dog, huh?"
