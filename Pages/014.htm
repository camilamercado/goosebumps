"We didn't mean to take the doll. W-we made a mistake," you stammer. "I'm sorry."

"A mistake?" The spirit bellows. He glares at you for a moment. Then, to your great relief, he nods in agreement.

"Well," he tells you, "anyone can make a mistake."

"They can?" you blurt out. "I mean, they can!" you add.

"Since it was just a mistake," the spirit continues, "you may leave. In fact, I will help you get home."

"Gee, thanks," you reply. You and Wendy nod happily.

The spirit lifts his hand, and a gust of wind blows through the cave. Magically you feel yourself lifted up and carried outside. You and Wendy are flying over the countryside. You're really going home!
