You have to stay. Aunt Emily and Uncle Jed may need your help - if they're still alive.

No! You tell yourself. They are alive. They have to be!

The twister moves away. You stumble back toward the farmhouse. Your stomach clenches as you head for the storm cellar. The door has been ripped off of it. You peer inside.

Gone! Aunt Emily and Uncle Jed - both gone.

Your eyes fill with tears. Could the twister have picked them up and tossed them away? If so, are they alive somewhere? How will you find them? What will you do now?

A shift in the wind stops your thoughts. Whoa!

The twister is coming back! You have to get out of here!

You spy your uncle's brick storehouse on the other side of the hill. Yes! You can hide there! You run so hard, the muscles in your legs burn, but there's no time to stop. At the top of the hill, you glance back.

Oh, no! Two strangers are walking right into the twister's path! You have to warn them! Then again, if you stop, you could be killed by the storm!
