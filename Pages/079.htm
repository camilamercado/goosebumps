Whoa! What is this? Your mind races. It sounds like - like a blackmail message!

Spin and Sally aren't trying to rescue your aunt and uncle! you slowly realize. They're using their invention to control the twister - and then they're threatening people into giving them money!

"Deliver the cash," the tape continues. "Or else."

You have to do something. You can't let Spin and Sally get away with this!

But what can you do? You could use the radio in the truck to call for help. But what if the police don't find you in time?

Instead, maybe you should try to wreck Spin and Sally's plan on your own. After all, the suit's controls are right in front of you. You could try to destroy them.

Even though you're not exactly sure how the controls work.
