"Fine." You take the doll from Wendy. "I'll do it." When you think about the angry wind spirit, your hands tremble a little. What will the spirit do to you? you wonder. But you steel yourself and place the doll on the rock shelf.

The doll begins to shine with a yellow glow. You gasp and take a step back.

"Check it out!" you cry to Wendy. But when you turn, she's gone. In her place is a ghostlike woman in Native American clothing. Somehow you know she's the wind spirit!

"Don't be scared of me," a familiar voice requests.

"Wendy?" you respond.

"Yes, it's me," she answers with a gentle smile.

"B-but - how..." you stammer.

"The doll was stolen," she explains. "And I lost control of the winds. I found the doll again, but only a mortal could return it. Thank you. Now I can repay you."

"Repay me?" you wonder.

She laughs. A breeze blows through the cavern. You're lifted off your feet and into the air.

"No!" you cry out in fear. "Stop!"
