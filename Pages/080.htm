You have to stay in the storehouse. Going outside would be crazy!

WHAM! A gust of wind slams against the building. You glance up. A piece of the roof flies off - then another.

The entire roof is ripped from the building! The world vanishes from view as billowing dust erases the ground, the sky, and the storehouse walls. You grab for something - anything - to hold on to. But it's too late. The wind scoops you off the ground and hurtles you through the air.

This is it! you think. I'm dead meat!

"Aaaah!" You turn head over heels, screaming as you tumble through the dust cloud. Your heart pounds hard against your rib cage. You're being carried into the twister!

But - wait a minute. You're not crashing into anything. The wind doesn't even hurt you. You're just - flying!
