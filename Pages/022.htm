Forget it, you think. I'm not going to let him get away with this.

"I won't let you destroy that town!" you shout to Spin.

Spin stares at you in shock. Then he lunges at you. You duck under his arm, but he comes after you again. You rush to the other side of the bank of monitors. Spin is right behind you.

The truck isn't that big. You know it's only a matter of time before he catches you!

"Spin?" Sally's voice breaks in over the radio intercom. "Spin! What's happening? I have control of the storm, but I need your data now!"

Spin stops. He glares at you. "You'll ruin everything!" he snaps. Then he sits in front of the control panel and starts punching buttons.

This is your big chance. But what should you do? Hit a few buttons and see what happens? Or maybe you could try to get Spin out of the truck. One thing's for sure. You have to decide on something - quick! Or Tally's Creek is done for!
