73

 

"Hurry!" Wendy shouts to you.

You struggle to make it up a rocky trail. A change in the breeze makes your heart stop. You don't have to look back to know the twister's closing in on you - again.

You reach the top of the trail - and see a huge cave. "Yes!" you cry. Inside, you can return the doll to its place with the wind spirit! Then you'll get Aunt Emily and Uncle Jed back - and that tornado will finally leave you alone!

The wind gusts, nearly knocking you over. Above you, a pine tree branch breaks off with a sickening crack. You race for the cave entrance and avoid the branch. But Wendy's not as fast. The branch slams into the side of her head. "Ohhh!" she groans. She collapses to the floor.

"Wendy!" you cry. But she's out cold. You drag her into the cave. Whew! She's still breathing. But the twister is raging outside the cave. You have to get rid of it!

You spy the Native American doll sticking out of Wendy's pocket. Should you try to wake Wendy so she can return the doll? Or should you take the doll and do it yourself?

If you try to wake her, go to PAGE 66.
