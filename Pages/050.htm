"No way!" you snap at Wendy. "I am not talking to a stupid carpet!"

"WHO ARE YOU CALLING STUPID?"

"Wha-?" You lose your grip on the rug. A mighty voice cuts through the storm. A bright blue face appears in the air in front of you.

"I told you it was magic!" Wendy shouts with glee.

Unreal! You blink a few times. But the face is still floating there. You take a deep breath. "Who are you?" you ask the face.

"Who do you think I am?" he answers with a sneer. "I am the genie of the carpet. And you said I was stupid!"

All you can do is stare, dumbfounded, at the genie's huge face. Why didn't you listen to Wendy?

"Oh, yeah," he adds in a gruff voice. "I am required to inform you that your wish is my command."

"Great!" you cry in a weak voice. You glance at the storm raging around you. "Genie, take us someplace safe!" you order.
