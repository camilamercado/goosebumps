You struggle against the restraints holding you in your seat. If only you could break free....

Wait a minute... if you twist your hand this way... yes! One hand slips out from under the strap. You undo all your buckles and reach for the door. You pull. No! It won't open!

You glance around the capsule. There are two buttons in the wall. One is marked STOP The other is marked PLAN B.

You have to do something, so you might as well try one of these buttons. But will you be lucky enough to pick the right one? To find out, flip a coin.

If it comes up heads, you push STOP.

Go to PAGE 95.

If it comes up tails, you push PLAN B.

Go to PAGE 33.
