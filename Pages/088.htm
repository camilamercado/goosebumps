You hold a press conference in your aunt and uncle's yard. "Ladies and gentlemen!" you announce. "I have amazing news!" A buzz of excitement goes through the crowd.

"The animals! They can talk," you tell the group. You smile. This is it - the moment when you become famous. You hope the photographers snap good shots of you.

One reporter smirks. "What? This kid's crazy!"

"Is this some kind of joke?" another reporter yells.

The crowd starts to leave in a hurry. Your aunt and uncle rush up to you.

"Wait-" you yell. "It's true! I can prove it." But no one listens. Your dreams of fame are deflated.

"Poor dear," your aunt says. She looks concerned. "We didn't realize the stress your little adventure caused. Don't worry. We're sending you to a hospital for a rest."

You try to explain, to protest. "But I'm not crazy! I really can talk to the animals!" you shout.

Too bad. Looks like you're going away for a long time. And the only person you'll have to talk to there is yourself.

THE END
