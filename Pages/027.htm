With a thundering blast the mountain blows its stack. Ash and cinders shoot high into the air. Oh, no! you realize. You're standing at the base of a volcano!

The ground shakes and rumbles. You want to run, but you're stuck to your place in fear.

The earth splits open beside you. A jet of red-hot molten lava explodes into the air. It gushes out of the ground and flows toward you. No way can you outrun it.

Oh, well. Looks like you're out of the frying pan... but into the fire!

THE END
