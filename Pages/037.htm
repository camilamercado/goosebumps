You won't leave your aunt and uncle, you decide.

You shake your fist in fury. "You can't scare us!" you shout into the wind.

"What do you mean us?" Yoyo barks. "I'm out of here!"

"Yoyo, wait!" you shout, but the little dog has already run off as fast as his short legs can carry him.

The wind rises around you. It's already hard to stand upright. Maybe Yoyo had the right idea.

"Yoyo!" you scream. "I'm coming!" You try to scramble off the pile of wreckage, but the wind knocks you down. You fall onto a jagged piece of wood. Splinters slice into your leg. Frantically you try to get to your feet, but you can't!

Rocks, branches, and dirt are being blown through the air. The funnel of swirling black dust is almost on top of you. You can barely breathe.

Whoops!

Looks like you should have gone with your dog. Because now any hope you had of saving your aunt and uncle is gone with the wind.

THE END
