What a nut! you think. The storm is not going to take a time-out just because this kid said -

You stop, midthought. The storm is slowing down! It's almost as if it did listen to the boy. This is too weird! The wind sets you down gently on the grass below.

Standing next to you are Wendy and the strange boy. Plus your aunt Emily and your uncle Jed!

"Why? How?" You stumble over the questions running through your head. Why are they here? How are they alive?

"Ahem." Someone behind you clears his throat.

You turn and see an old man standing behind you. He's wearing a lab coat. His long white hair stands straight up from his head.

"I think I can clear up your confusion," he says.

Oh, really? you think. This should be a good one.
