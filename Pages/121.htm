"Here!" you shout. You throw the doll into the storm. You watch as the wind tears it to bits. Huh? you think. Why is the whirlwind doing that?

Wendy blinks and opens her eyes. Just in time to see the doll shredded. "No! What did you do?" she yells.

A feeling of dread spreads over you. "I-I gave the doll back to the wind spirit," you stammer.

"You jerk!" Wendy cries. She struggles to get up. "The twister isn't the wind spirit, it's controlled by the wind spirit. Its only purpose was to get us back here. Now, with the doll destroyed, the twister can do whatever it wants!"

"What?" you cry. But your words are drowned out by the laughing voice of the tornado.

"I am free!" the storm thunders. "Finally! Free to do as I wish! And now I wish to destroy the two of you!"

The wind in the small cave howls louder. You feel the whirlwind's unbelievable power. In just a few moments it will crush your body against the rock walls.

Too bad. And you thought getting out of here was going to be a breeze!

THE END
