You're sure they want you to stop! There must be something in front of you. Something you can't see - like a ditch or a fence. You slam on the brake. Hard.

Unfortunately, since you've never driven a car before, you slam on the brake too hard. Which sends you flying toward the windshield.

And, oops! In all the confusion you forgot to buckle your seat belt. A major no-no.

In a second you're through the windshield and flying right into the oncoming tornado.

You should never have thought you could drive.

Oh, well. Those are the brakes!

THE END
