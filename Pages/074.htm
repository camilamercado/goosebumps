Something small and sharp slams into the side of your head.

Yeow! A bird stabbed you with its beak!

Pain shoots through your right leg. You reach down and grab something small and furry. A squirrel is biting you!

Animals of every size and shape rush at you. You jump backwards and fall outside.

Where is Yoyo? you wonder. He could talk to these animals. He could save you! But your dog is nowhere in sight.

The huge brown bear begins to charge at you..

No! He's coming to get you!

You bolt from the house. But you know you can't outrun all these beasts! You have to hide!

In the distance you spot a school building. Outside it is a yellow school bus.

The bus is closer than the school. You can reach it in a few seconds. But can it protect you from the animals?

Maybe you should head for the school instead!
