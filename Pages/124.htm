You, a movie star? This guy's got to be nuts!

"I don't know anything about acting," you admit.

"Too bad." Sealburg shrugs. "You could have been big. Well, Jed and Em, I guess it's just you."

"Jed and Em?" you repeat, confused.

"Yup," your uncle confirms. "Mr. Sealburg wants to make us movie stars too. We're leaving for Hollywood right now."

"On his private jet," your aunt Emily chimes in. She gives you a peck on the cheek and follows your uncle into a black limousine.

"Now, don't forget to milk the cows and feed the chickens," she reminds you.

"Cows? Chickens?" you repeat.

You realize you've lost your big chance to be rich and famous. Not only that, you have to take care of the farm all by yourself. As the limo pulls off in a cloud of dust, you start to run after it.

"Hey!" you shout. "What about me?"

Your uncle pokes his head out the window. "Don't worry. If you're ever in Hollywood, we'll do lunch!"

THE END
