There's no way you can make a radio call without Spin hearing you. You have to destroy their controls!

Luckily, Spin is talking to Sally - who's now inside the twister - on the two-way radio.

You study the instruments - and notice a small screen in front of you. It shows the path of the storm. Next to it is a screen that looks like a video game. And there's a joystick in front of it. That must be the controller for the tornado!

You can tell at a glance that the twister is definitely headed for Tully's Creek, the town from the videotape.

Hey! You have friends in that town! You can't let them die!

You grab the joystick. Maybe you can direct the storm away!

"What are you doing?"

Spin's harsh voice makes you jump out of your seat. He's standing over you, and he looks like he's angry - very angry!
