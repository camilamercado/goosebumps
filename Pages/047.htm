This is no time to learn how to drive, you tell yourself. You keep running toward the couple.

When you get close enough, you scream, "Run! Run for your lives!" Sweat trickles down your face, stinging your eyes. But you keep running in their direction. You point toward the approaching storm.

The man and woman turn and stare at you. At first they look startled to see you. But then they frown and shake their heads.

Whoa. Nice reception for someone who was about to save your life, you think.

"Thanks," the woman scolds you. "You ruined our shot!"

"What? Your shot?" you repeat. You follow the woman's gaze down the hillside - and see a camera crew!
