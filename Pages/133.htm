You tread water like mad. You have to keep your head afloat! Yoyo dog-paddles next to you, trying to do the same.

"I thought you animals could predict natural disasters!" you cry to your dog. "What happened?"

"Some things I'm better at than others." Yoyo shrugs. "Now, forest fires, I get right every time. But when it comes to floods, I'm a big washout!"
