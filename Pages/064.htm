You realize that Yoyo is trying to lead you to safety. You turn and run after him.

But now the twister seems to be following you. You race after your trusty dog.

Yoyo scrambles a little way ahead to an abandoned farmhouse by the road. He barks and jumps up and down.

You realize he wants you to go inside the house. But that building is now a wreck. It can barely stand up. How can it shelter you from a twister?

You could keep running. But you're so tired, you don't know how much farther you can go.
