The truck roars off, and soon you're back at the wrecked house. You watch helplessly as rescue teams arrive with heavy equipment. You stand to one side, numb with exhaustion.

"We've found them!" a rescuer shouts. "They're okay!"

A state trooper comes up to you. "We'll have them out in a little while," he declares. "How did you know they were there?"

"My dog -" you begin. But the thought of Yoyo lying alone in that pit makes you break down in tears. You run for the storm cellar. You have to see if he's still there. If the rescue team won't help you, you'll do it alone.

The twister is gone and the day is clear. You stumble your way over fallen trees and through puddles. Covered in mud, legs weak, you reach the pit. With your last bit of strength you go up to the edge and gaze down. Your heart leaps in happiness. It's Yoyo!
