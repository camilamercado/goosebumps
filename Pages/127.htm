"Congratulations!" a voice shouts.

You glance around and see Spin on a monitor next to you.

"You pressed PLAN B! That activates another one of our illegal experiments!" he tells you.

Your heart sinks. What have you done? "What do you mean?" you ask.

"Our other experiment, where we send someone into space and see if they can survive a trip to Pluto and back," he explains.

"PLUTO?" you shriek. "That's the farthest planet from Earth!"

"That's right," Spin says. "So just sit back and relax. In this craft the trip should only take about twenty years."

Your stomach clenches. "Twenty years?" you croak.

"Don't worry," Spin assures you. "There's plenty of food on board. I hope you like peanut butter."

As you zoom toward the outer reaches of the solar system, you can't help but think, peanut butter's not so bad. It goes with the huge jam I'm in!

THE END.
