69

 

The carpet dives sharply. Aaah! You're falling to the ground!

You start talking. What have you got to lose?

"Magic carpet!" you shout. "Take us to safety!"

You grip the fringe of the rug with all your strength. Please let it work, you pray. Please let the carpet hear me.

You feel the rug shake and shift under you. It begins to climb again. Yes! It seems to be following your command!

But you didn't command it to flip upside down!

"Yowwww!" you howl as the rug turns over. It tumbles to the ground. And so do you and Wendy! Talking to the rug didn't do a thing. The tornado is in charge after all!

Screaming, the two of you hurtle to the earth. In another few seconds you'll just be a memory.

Boy, you think. This time you sure had the rug pulled out from under you!

THE END
