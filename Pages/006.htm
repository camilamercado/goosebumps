Well, duh! Of course you accept! You're going to be a STAR!

The next few days fly by. Before you know it, Sealburg puts you on a plane to Los Angeles. You stay at the fanciest hotel. And eat the fanciest meals. Your face is on every magazine cover. You're hot! And you haven't even made a movie yet! Everything is beyond your wildest dreams.

Finally the big day comes. You head to the set to shoot your first movie. It's called Cyclone.

"Stand right over there," Sealburg orders. He points to a spot in front of the camera. You stride to the spot and glance behind you. Yikes! About a foot away is a pit filled with sharp, jagged metal.

"That stuff is fake, right?" you ask.

"No, it's real," the director answers with a frown. "We need everything to look as convincing as possible. Now, when we turn on the wind machine, just act scared."

Sounds easy, you think.

"Ready?" Sealburg cries. "ACTION!"
