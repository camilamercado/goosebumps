"Sorry," you mutter to the boy. "I'm out of here."

You stretch your body as far over as you can. You reach your hand out and grab the rope. Yes! You snag it!

Yeow! Your arm is nearly jerked out of its socket! The wind is tugging at you, trying to tear you away from the rope. You fight its force to get your other hand and your feet around the rope.

The rope is almost ten inches thick. It sways a bit in the wind, but it seems sturdy.

WHACK! A heavy piece of wood smacks you in the arm. Yikes! You've got to move - fast.

Time to see where this rope goes.

You gaze down at the thick, swirling tornado beneath you. But when you look up, the sky is lighter.

Hey! The rope goes above the storm. You frown. This is totally weird. Then again, it's totally good news for you. Maybe you can climb up and hang out there until the twister moves on.

One foot at a time, you begin to haul yourself up the green rope. Carefully. Because one slip could mean your death!
