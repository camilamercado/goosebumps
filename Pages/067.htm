"Come on," you urge Yoyo. "We have to get help!"

"No kidding," he growls.

The two of you run down the road. The sky grows darker every second.

"This way!" you shout when you reach a turn in the road. "I think there are some buildings up ahead."

You wheel around. Yoyo's gone!

"Yoyo!" you shout. The wind is rising, and the trees are swaying wildly. Then you hear a faint barking.

" I'm down here!" Yoyo calls.

You stumble in your pooch's direction, and nearly fall into a deep storm cellar. It's all that remains of a house torn away by the twister. You lean over and see Yoyo lying at the bottom of the cellar.

"I can't get out!" he calls up.

You look at the sky. You may not have enough time to get down there and lift Yoyo out. But if you leave him, he'll be killed.
