You turn to Wendy, who is gliding beside you. "I told you!" you brag. "All we had to do is say we made a mistake!"

"Yeah... well," she mumbles under her breath.

"Yup, as long we get home we..." Your words catch in your throat. The wind carrying you is dying away! You're beginning to drop.

"What's going on?" Wendy shouts. The two of you plummet downward.

"Wind spirit!" you cry. "Help!"

As if by command, the wind spirit shows up next to you.

"What's wrong?" he asks.

"What's wrong?" you scream. "We're starting to fall. We're going to be killed!"

"Sorry," the wind spirit says with a fiendish grin. "I must have turned off the wind. Oops. Looks like, just like you, I made a mistake."

THE END
