"We just didn't know enough about your culture," you admit. "But we'd sure like to learn."

The anger on the spirit's face slowly melts.

"Really?" he asks. "Very well then."

All right! The spirit believes you! A gust of wind sweeps you and Wendy off your feet. The cavern vanishes in a blur.

THUMP! You and Wendy are plunked down in a meadow at the edge of a forest. Nice, you think. Until you notice what Wendy is wearing. She's dressed in a beaded leather outfit. You glance down. You're dressed that way too! The wind spirit hovers in the air next to you.

"Down that hill is a village," he informs you. "Go and live with the people there. Learn our culture from them. I have brought you to the past, before Europeans came to our land. Have a wonderful life here."

"The past! But-" you start to argue.

"You said you wished to learn," the spirit tells you. He vanishes.

Learning about the wind spirit's tribe will be a lot of work, but, hey, you've got about five centuries to do it in!

THE END
