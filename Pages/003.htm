"I can't!"you shout back. "Not without Yoyo!"
Yoyo - your trusty little black dog.
Where is he? He usually follows you everywhere.
You turn to go find him, but dust clogs the air. You can barely see! The wind tugs at your clothes. It's hard even to stay on your feet.
Uncle Jed's strong hand lands on your shoulder.
"Come on, now," he cries in your ear. "There's no time!"
But over the fierce howling of the wind, you think you hear a faint noise. Is it...?
It is!
It's Yoyo's bark!
