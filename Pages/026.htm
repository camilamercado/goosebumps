You can't try to save those people. It's too risky. You make a mad dash for the shelter of the brick storehouse.

The wind grows stronger. It slams against your chest - knocking the air out of you.

I'll never make it! you think. But fear drives you forward. Your hand closes around the handle of the storehouse door. You pull with all your might.

WHAM! The door flies open as the wind catches it. You feel yourself being lifted off your feet.

"NO!" you scream. From inside the storehouse a slender hand reaches out, grabs you, and pulls you inside.

"Whew," you say. "Thanks. That was close!" You look up to see who helped you. It's a girl just about your age. Her long brown hair is tangled around her dirty face.

"Come on!" she shouts. "Help me close the door!"

Together you pull the heavy wooden door shut. You collapse against a wall, breathing heavily.

"Who are you?" you gasp.

"I'm Wendy," she shouts above the roaring wind. "And I'm the reason the twister is here!"
