"Wow!" you whisper. The carpet you and Wendy are sitting on is levitating in the air.

You force yourself to peek over the fringed edge of the rug. Yikes! The storm sucks you outside. It lifts you way up. You're hundreds of feet in the air.

Your heart clangs against your rib cage. You grip the edges of the carpet tightly. This is totally terrifying!

"It's magic!" Wendy cries with glee. "A magic carpet!"

You scowl at her. What does she think this is, a movie?

"All we have to do is tell the carpet where to go and it will take us there," Wendy insists.

Wow. This girl has totally lost it. First she thinks the "wind spirit" is after her. Now she wants you to talk to a carpet!

But, hey - the twister did seem to be following her. Maybe she's right about the wind spirit. Maybe it will keep sending the twister after you until Wendy returns the doll to the cave.

Maybe talking to the carpet isn't such a crazy idea.
