You know the twister looks dangerous, but you can't help thinking of what it did to your aunt and uncle.

You feel anger well up inside of you. "How can you say people don't matter?" you yell at the storm. "You hurt two people who matter very much - to me! You have to stop!"

"Forget it!" the storm spits out the words. "A twister goes where it wants, blows down what it wants. And hurts anyone it wants. Nothing can stop me! Especially not a puny human!"

The storm opens its mouth. Wide.
