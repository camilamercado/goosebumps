You know that bus isn't sturdy enough to stop all the animals! Especially the bear and the horse!

You sprint like crazy to the school building.

Behind you, a dog snaps at your legs. Your heart thuds in your chest. Your breath comes in painful gasps.

You try the school door - it's open! You dive inside and lock it.

Pounding sounds from the outside. It's just a matter of time, you realize, before the animals break inside. You moan softly, wondering what to do next.

Still gasping for breath, you run blindly down the school hallway. You search for something, anything, that will help you.

You throw open one door after another. All you find are empty classrooms. Soon you reach the last door on the hall. Frantically you throw it open.

And gasp at what you see.

The room is filled with animals!
