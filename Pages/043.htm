"You're the what?" You thought you heard the voice say it was the storm. But, that's impossible - right?

There's only one way to find out the truth, you realize - talk back. You turn off your microphone. You don't want Sally and Spin to think you're crazy.

You clear your throat. "You're the storm?" you ask.

"Yes," the voice answers. The air in front of you goes from black to bright blue. In the middle you see - a face! Its glowing eyes narrow at you suspiciously: Its mouth scowls at you. And it speaks!

"I am the storm!" it declares. As it talks you can see rows of sharp teeth sticking out of its mouth.

"What's going on up there?" Sally's voice demands from the speaker in your ear. "Report!"

What can you say? If you tell her you're talking to the storm, she'll think you're crazy and end the mission. But then, maybe that's not such a bad thing. This storm doesn't look too friendly.
