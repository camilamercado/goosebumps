The animals won't give you a chance to talk, you realize. You have to show them you can be trusted.

You throw open all the doors on the mouse cages.

Then you tip over the tanks and let dozens of frogs and lizards out into the world.

The angry animals burst into the lab.

"This human freed us," a white lab rat squeaks.

You smile and nod in agreement. See? I'm a nice human, you think.

"You know what this means?" the horse rumbles.

"Yes," the bear growls. He bares his teeth at you. "We have more help! Kill the human!"

Talk about ungrateful! The white lab rat and the rest of his friends turn on you. They fling their small bodies at your feet and ankles. Ouch! You feel the sharp stings of dozens of tiny teeth and claws. As you bend down to knock them away, you stare face-to-face at the snarling bear. Uh-oh!

Looks like you've come to an ending that's a little more than you can bear.

THE END
