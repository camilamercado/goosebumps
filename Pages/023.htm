You have to stay in the cave until you figure out what to do, you decide.

"You have to remember, Wendy," you insist. "Or we're up against a wall here."

"Did you say wall?" Wendy asks.

You shrug. "Yeah. So what?"

"So now I remember what to do - thanks to you!" Wendy takes the doll from you and places it in a small hollow in the cave wall. It fits inside perfectly.

"Here's your doll back," Wendy shouts. "Sorry. My fault."

You jump in surprise as a ghostly being pops into view. He's dressed in traditional Native American garb. The spirit is almost transparent. But you can still tell he's angry.

"The wind spirit," Wendy whispers.

The figure looms over you. No! What's he going to do?

The spirit's frown melts into a smile. "You have done well," he declares. "I will give you a reward."

Wow! A reward! You've never felt so relieved.

The spirit raises his hand, and the wind blowing through the cave lifts you and Wendy!
