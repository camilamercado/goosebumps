You have to find out who this boy is - and what he and Wendy are talking about, you decide. Besides, who knows where that rope goes.

"What do you mean, 'play'?" you shout to the boy over the sound of the storm.

"Hold on!" the boy shouts. "I can't hear you!"

No kidding, you think. We're in the middle of a tornado!

"Hey!" the boy yells. "Time-out! Stop blowing ! I can't hear him!"

Time-out? What does he think he's doing? you wonder. Does he really think the storm will listen to him?
