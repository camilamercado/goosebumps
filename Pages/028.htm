You can't believe it! The whole disaster is a fake! The man and woman you tried to save are actors! In some stupid tornado movie!

You glance around at all the camera equipment and gasp in surprise. Standing next to a huge wind machine are Uncle Jed and Aunt Emily! They're alive! You jog over to them.

Your aunt puts her arm across your shoulder. "Did we forget to tell you the movie crew was coming today?"

"B-b-but the twister..." you stammer. "You acted scared."

"Just practicing our parts," your uncle declares.

"Parts?" you exclaim. "I thought you were both dead!"

Before your aunt and uncle can explain, a tall, bearded man wearing a baseball cap strides up to you.

"Meet Mr. Sealburg, our director," your aunt exclaims.

"Kid, you were super!" he cries. "Loved the way you acted scared. I'm going to make you a major star!"

What? You're no actor. How can you be in the movies?

"I have to know right now!" the world's greatest director states. "Are you in my movie or not?"

If you accept the part, go to PAGE 6.
