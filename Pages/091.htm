You grab the rope.

"We have to wait it out!" you order Wendy. The two of you stop at a large boulder and sit down with your backs against it. Then you pass the rope around the boulder and your waists, tying it tightly.

"We'll be okay now!" you shout. You cross your fingers, hoping you're right.

The wind continues to gust, but you and Wendy are fine.

It's working, you think.

The shrieking of the tornado grows even louder. Next to you, the walls of the cave begin to shake. Bits of stone crumble off the roof and start to fall around you.

"The twister is breaking up the mountain!" Wendy cries. "We'll be buried alive!"

No! You can't stay here! Frantically you tug at the rope, trying to get free. But the knot is stuck!

Too bad, looks like some rocks are going to roll all over you!

THE END
