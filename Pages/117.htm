You know your suit can't go fast enough to escape the twister. You have to trick it somehow. You just hope this works.

"I know you want to kill me," you tell the storm. "But please, please - whatever you do, don't take me to the mountains! Destroy me here!" It's easy to sound scared, since you are. Very.

The twister grins wickedly. "I think I will destroy you over the mountains. I am the storm! I can do whatever I want!"

You feel the black funnel moving, carrying you across the plains. Through swirling dust you make out a small mountain range a few miles away.

"Ha!" the tornado laughs. "See how fast I can go! We will be at the mountains in a flash!"

Good! That's what you're counting on.
