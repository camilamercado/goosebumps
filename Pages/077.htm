You think of the wind spirit. What if it's so angry, it decides to punish whoever took the doll?

"No way!" you tell Wendy. "You took it. You return it!"

Trembling, Wendy nods and turns toward the shrine. She places the small doll in an empty spot on the shelf.

A huge gust of wind blasts through the depths of the cave. You and Wendy step back. Your heart starts pounding double-time.

The doll! You realize. It's beginning to move!

You stand rooted to the spot in shock. The small corn-husk doll grows larger - until it towers over you.

And as it grows it changes. It's no longer a doll but the shimmering, transparent figure of a person.

"The wind spirit!" you gasp. It has taken the form of a Native American warrior!

The warrior gazes down, points at you with a ghostly hand, and opens his mouth to speak.
