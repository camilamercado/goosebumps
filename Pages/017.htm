Yes, you decide. You'll get Spin out of the truck - somehow. You glance at him... and notice something. His chair is on wheels - and it's near the end of the truck!

Near the wide-open back door! All you have to do is...

You run at the chair. "Ooof!" You throw your weight against it. It rolls across the floor of the truck and out the back - with Spin still on it!

"Hey!" he shouts. He thuds onto the dirt below.

You slam the doors closed and bolt them from the inside. Whew! You're safe.

"Spin? Spin, what's going on?" Sally's voice sounds frantic over the intercom. You race to the controls.

"Spin isn't here," you say into the microphone. "And your plans for blackmail are through!"

"You saw the tape?" Sally asks, her voice tense.

"That's right!" you cry. "Now I'm reeling you in!"

Your hand reaches for the button marked CABLE CONTROL. You press it.

"No!" Sally cries. "Don't bring me in! I - I found your aunt and uncle!"
