You won't be able to save your aunt and uncle if the bridge breaks and you drown in the river.

"Come on!" you shout to Yoyo. "Let's find the next bridge."

You run hard, with Yoyo scampering at your side. The riverbank is wet and muddy. You slip and slide in the muck. It's slow going. But finally you see the other bridge, not far in the distance.

"Okay, let's cross," you tell Yoyo. You hit the bridge. In the middle of the bridge's span, Yoyo stops in his tracks. "What's wrong, boy?" you ask.

But he doesn't answer. You follow his gaze upstream and see a solid wall of water, thirty feet high, rushing down the river at you. A flash flood!

You want to run off the bridge to safety, but your feet are frozen in place. The huge wave looms higher above you.

The water hits you. A cold shock paralyzes your body. The bridge gives way with a sickening crack.

There's no escape. You and Yoyo are going to drown!
