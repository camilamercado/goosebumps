How are you going to convince Sally and Spin that you're talking to a twister? You hardly believe it yourself.

"It's nothing," you say into your microphone. "I was just, uh, singing."

"Well, pay attention!" Sally scolds. "Your life depends on it."

"Okay!" you agree. Then you flip off the microphone. You take a deep breath. Even though you feel like an idiot doing it, you decide to talk with the twister.

You think about your aunt and uncle, and there's only one question on your mind. "Why do you destroy things?" you ask. "Why do you hurt people?"

The storm grins at you, showing all its pointed, razor-sharp teeth. "People are not very important to me."

GULP! That doesn't sound too promising.
