You've got to convince the animals to reveal their secret. The safety of humankind may depend on it!

"Uh, excuse me," you mumble.

All the animals turn and stare at you. A cold sweat breaks out on your forehead.

"I couldn't help overhearing you," you stammer.

You pause to take a breath. There's silence in the room, except for the sound of the wind outside.

"So you all can t-t-talk!" you stammer. "Pretty cool. So anyway, I was thinking, about this storm-warning thing. If you guys would consider letting us humans know..."

Your voice trails off. The animals haven't made a peep or moved a muscle. Maybe they're thinking it over.

"A human," the dog growls. "A human heard us!"

"Kill it!" the bear roars.

Whoops! Maybe not.
