Spin and Sally force you into the rocket. They strap you into a seat in the nose cone.

"Why?" you ask. "Why are you doing this to me?"

"Our research was ended by the government," Sally explains. "They thought it was too dangerous." She rolls her eyes. "For months, we had no test subjects. But when you tried to save us from the twister, we knew you were a perfect human subject - someone brave enough in the face of the storm to try out our theory."

No! These guys aren't just crazy, you realize. They're criminals.

"We weren't lying," Spin explains. "We do think someone can survive inside the twister. We just need you to prove it!"

"I don't want to!" you scream. "Let me go!"

Spin doesn't listen. He shuts the heavy steel door. It bolts closed with a sickening CLANK!
