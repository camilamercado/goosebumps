You shudder. Talking animals? You can't believe it.

You realize with growing amazement that the deep voice belongs to a huge brown bear.

"Stupid humans," it roars. "They couldn't tell the twister was coming until it almost hit. Then they ran into their holes in the ground."

"They have lost their connection with the earth - and with the sky," twitters a bird.

"Yes, they call us dumb animals," a squirrel chimes in. "But we always know when a storm or earthquake is coming."

"Or a flood," whinnies a horse. "If they listened to us, we could save them a lot of trouble."

"But we won't help them," the bear adds. "Not after the way they've treated us."

"Never!" a chorus of voices cries.

This is too completely unbelievable. You creep closer to hear some more.
