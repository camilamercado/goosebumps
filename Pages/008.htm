"Alive?" you repeat.

"Yes," Spin insists. "If the tornado did sweep them up, as you say it did, it's possible they're in a previously undiscovered zone we found inside the tornado. It's safe there. But you're trapped in the storm. With no way to get out."

"Until now," Sally adds. "We've found a way to get inside that safe zone. In fact, we were about to test it out. If you help us, there's a chance we might find your aunt and uncle in the twister."

You stare at the two scientists. Your head is whirling with happiness. Maybe you can save your aunt and uncle!

But, then again, you just met Spin and Sally. How do you know you can trust them?

I mean, a survival zone inside a tornado? How do you know they're not making it up?

"If we're going to try, we have to act fast," Sally tells you. "While the twister is still active!"
