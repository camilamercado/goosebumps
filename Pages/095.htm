You push the button marked STOP.

You hear a computer voice from a speaker in the wall. "Self-destruct program selected. Countdown beginning now"

"Self-destruct...?" you whisper. Your stomach flops when you realize your mistake. The STOP button stops the rocket by destroying it! And you're trapped inside!

You're a goner.

"Help!" you scream.

"Ten seconds until detonation," the voice says. "Nine, eight, seven..."

Oh, well. At least you're ending this adventure with a real BANG.

THE END
