You'd never be able to force Spin off the truck. You decide to try to mess with the controls!

You spot a piece of metal pipe lying on the truck's floor. Hey. That gives you an idea. You pick up the pipe and dart over to the control panels.

You swing the pipe down - ZZZZZT! Sparks fly everywhere.

The intercom sputters. "My suit - losing power!" Sally shouts. Her voice sounds panicky. "Get me back!"

"No! You wrecked our plan!" Spin wails at you.

He opens the truck's back door and gasps. You glance outside. Oh, no! The twister is coming right at you!

Spin shoves you out of the truck. "Since you made us lose control of the storm, you can face the consequences!"

He unties Sally's tether from the truck. The rope snaps away. It flies off into the heart of the storm.

"See you around, Sally," Spin mutters. You can't believe it! Spin just left his own partner inside the tornado!

He lunges into the driver's seat. The truck's engine roars and Spin pulls away - leaving you to face the storm - alone!
