Stop these crooks by yourself? No way! Puhlease! But if you call for help, maybe the police can nab them.

There's a cellular phone on the table next to you. Holding your breath, you pick it up.

Spin stays focused on his side of the control panel. Whew! You turn the phone on.

A large hand clamps down on your shoulder.

Oh, no! You blew it!

"I told Sally we'd never get you to help us." It's Spin. He throws you up against one of the walls of the truck. He ties your hands. The cords bite into your wrists. Then he sits you down and binds you to a chair.

"Now," he gloats, "watch us do what no one has ever dreamed of. We will control a tornado!"
