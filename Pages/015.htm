15

 

You have to return the doll yourself. There's no time to lose! You grab the wind doll from Wendy's pocket and run farther into the cave.

After just a few steps it becomes pitch-black. You have to feel your way forward with outstretched hands.

"Whoa!" you cry. The floor gives way beneath you!

You drop to the ground with a thud. Then you hear something that makes your blood run cold.

A rattle.

Something cold and smooth glides over your leg. You have to bite your lip to keep from screaming. You hear another rattle - then another.

You realize the horrible truth. Somehow you've landed in a nest of snakes!

You feel fangs sink into your leg with a sharp, stabbing pain. Instantly your leg goes numb.

"No!" you wail. "No-o-o-o-o!"

The deadly coldness starts to spread up your leg and through your body.

Face it. You're doomed. You've been killed by a snake. And that really bites!

THE END
