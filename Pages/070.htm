You want to do what's best for your aunt and uncle, but you just don't think you can handle flying into a twister.

"I think I'd better help out on the ground," you decide.

"That's probably the best choice," Sally replies. She puts her arm on your shoulder. "I'll squeeze into the suit and you can help Spin with the controls."

You help Spin and Sally load all their equipment into a truck. Then Spin checks a screen in the back of the truck.

"Yes!" he shouts. "The twister is still active!" He jumps into the driver's seat. "I saw it on the radar. It's just a few miles away! Let's chase it!"

The three of you roar off in the truck and race down a country road. Soon you can see the black funnel of the twister not far away. It's headed right for you.

Spin stops the truck, and Sally climbs into the suit.

"Wish me luck," she says. She clamps on the helmet.
