You gaze at your aunt and uncle. They're alive. They're unharmed. And they're even smiling!

You guess they should be smiling - you're all getting a new house out of this.

You stare at Neederlander. He seems like a nice enough guy -and that Twister Pack does have you curious....

"Okay," you decide. "I'll play. But only if I get to be 'it.' "

Neederlander agrees. He instructs you on how to use the backpack, and you blast off.

Woo-hoo! Being a tornado is fun! Looks like you, Peter, Neederlander, and Wendy are going to have lots of fun "doing the twist" from now on.
