You don't know if you can trick the twister, and you're not going to try.

You hit the thruster button on the outside of the suit. You're jerked upward by the jet pack on your back. Using the controls Sally showed you, you steer to the storm's edge.

Your heart beats wildly. It's working! Soon you'll be free.

"Going somewhere?" the twister snarls. With a sickening crack, your suit slams against a wall of wind. Your head bounces against the inside of the helmet. The jets scream, then die. They're no match for the force of a tornado.

"You cannot escape my grasp!" the twister shouts. "No one who enters the twister ever leaves - alive!"

The wind spins even faster, tearing at your suit. The twister's blue face comes closer to you. Its large mouth opens wide.

You feel yourself being pushed toward it. Into its sharp teeth! The huge monster clamps its jaws down on you.

Too bad. Looks like yelling at the twister has cost you your life. If you hadn't opened your mouth and been such a windbag, you might have survived.

THE END
