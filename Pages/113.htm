We can't give up! We have to get to the back of the cave. It'll be safer there, you reason.

You grab Wendy's arm, and the two of you push farther down the tunnel. Soon the force of the wind dies away.

"We made it!" you gasp. You pant for breath.

"Not yet!" Wendy replies. "We have to find the shrine - the place where I found the doll." She glances to the right. "There it is!"

Along one wall of the cavern is a stone shelf. It's lined with bits of old clay pottery, arrows, feathers, and other tribal relics. Wendy walks to the shrine, with the small doll in her hand.

"I can't." She turns to you, shaking. "You do it."

You start to take the doll from Wendy and stop short.

Wait a minute, you think. Wendy stole this doll from a wind spirit. What if the spirit is angry? If you return the doll, maybe the wind spirit will think you stole it. You don't want to get involved with that, do you?

Then again, maybe it will be better if you just return the doll and get this over with.
