A giant fan begins blowing on you. "Look frightened!" Sealburg instructs.

You form your mouth into a little "0" of fear.

"Not enough!" Sealburg shouts. "Turn up the machine!"

The wind grows stronger. Hey, wait a minute! The wind is actually pushing you toward the pit full of sharp metal!

"Good!" the director cries. "Now turn the wind machine up all the way!"

"Whoooa!" you cry. The wind shoves you back - hard. You grab for a nearby tree and hang on Your feet are inches away from the pit full of jagged metal!

"Help!" you shout.

"Great!" Sealburg claps his hands. "Incredible!"

He thinks you're still acting. But you're not! And your fingers are losing their grip. Another few seconds and you'll be blown onto the metal blades.

"Yes!" Sealburg shouts. "This performance will win me my Oscar!"

Great, you think. Maybe I'll win an Oscar too. For Best Scream Play!

THE END
